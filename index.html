About Us

Chire Industrial Power Solutions (CIPS) is a professional industrial electrical engineering company located in Athi River Industrial Area, Kenya. We specialize in industrial electrical maintenance, machine troubleshooting, industrial installations, power distribution systems, automation support, and supply of industrial electrical spare parts. Our goal is to help industries reduce downtime, increase productivity and maintain reliable electrical operations.


Vision

To become Kenya’s most trusted provider of industrial electrical maintenance and power solutions.


Mission

To provide reliable, efficient and affordable industrial electrical services that improve safety, productivity and operational excellence.
